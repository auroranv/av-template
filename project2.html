Fiction short (25 min)
Graduation film
Ezra gets confronted by the death of his best friend when he is asked by their mother to recreate him in an AI. While he trains the AI bot he goes through their shared memories but finds himself in between his guilt of what has happened and his responsibility as an AI trainer: is it right to change someone in order to “save” them?
